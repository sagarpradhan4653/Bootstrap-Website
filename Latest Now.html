Specifications

This Slowhand Series Acoustic guitar by Kadence is the new generation high quality instrument, finely crafted to give you the best sound and complete tonal control. It is loaded with features that you would expect in more expensive guitars. Designed in premium gloss finish Mahogany body and back with Indian Rosewood fret board and bridge, this beauty has a solid wood spruce top and is equipped with an in-built equalizer. The Kadence signature scoop, which works like a demi-cutaway, accommodates even the most demanding playing styles. The decorative in-lays subtly showcase the Kadence symbol and the high quality decal marks the series name, placed uniquely next to the strings on the body.
